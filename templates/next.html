COVID-19 Information

Symptoms of COVID-19

COVID-19 can cause a range of symptoms. Some of the most common symptoms include:

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of sense of smell or taste

If you experience any of these symptoms, please contact your healthcare provider immediately.

Precautions to Take

There are several precautions that you can take to prevent the spread of COVID-19. These include:

  • Wearing a mask when in public
  • Washing your hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
  • Maintaining a distance of at least 6 feet from others
  • Avoiding large gatherings and crowds

Remember, by taking these precautions, you can help protect yourself and others from COVID-19.
